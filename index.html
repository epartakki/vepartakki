About Us

We are sisters Eleni Partakki and Victoria Partakki, daughters of the late Demetris Partakkis — a great man, a farmer, and our lifelong inspiration. Guided by his values of hard work, integrity, and love for the land, we continue his legacy through our shared passion for both farming and engineering.

Our business is a unique blend of agriculture and innovation. On our farm, we produce fresh, locally grown goods with care and respect for the environment. In our engineering space, we design and build practical tools, systems, and workshops that empower farmers, makers, and our local community.

Our Vision

By combining traditional farming wisdom with modern engineering, we aim to create a sustainable future where food production and technology go hand in hand. Our vision is to be more than just a farm or a workshop—we strive to be a hub of creativity, problem-solving, and community-driven growth.

What We Do

  • Grow fresh, seasonal produce with sustainable practices.
  • Design and build engineering solutions for farmers and makers.
  • Host workshops and hands-on learning experiences.
  • Foster innovation in our community through collaboration.
